Privacy Policy

How Echo Loop handles the small amount of data involved in running an instant-play browser arcade.

Last updated: July 2026

01Overview

Echo Loop ("we", "us") is operated by Echo Loop Games Ltd.. This policy explains what information we collect when you use our website and games, and how we use it.

We are committed to collecting as little personal data as possible. You can browse and play without creating an account.

02Information we collect

Usage data: anonymous, aggregated information such as which games are opened and general device type, used to improve the arcade.

Contact data: if you email us or use the contact form, we receive the details you choose to share so we can reply.

03How we use information

To operate, maintain, and improve the site and its games.

To respond to your questions and feedback.

We do not sell your personal information.

04Cookies

We use a minimal set of cookies to make the site work and to understand aggregate usage. See our Cookie Policy for details.

05Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@echoloop.gg.

06Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach Echo Loop Games Ltd. at hello@echoloop.gg.
